What is Church.IO?

We are a community of developers, designers, and users who believes church software is too expensive and difficult to use. We're working to change that by creating simple, easy-to-use tools and giving them away.

Get involved by joining the mailing list and/or hopping into the IRC chat room #church.io on freenode and saying hi.
