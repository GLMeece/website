What is Church.IO?

Church software is too expensive and too difficult to use. We hope to change that by creating simple, easy-to-use tools and giving them away to any church who needs them.

Church.IO is a community of developers and designers working to affect the world and help churches everywhere by giving away our work.

Get involved by joining the mailing list and/or hopping into the IRC chat room #church.io on freenode.
